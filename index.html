Bold and Italic

The texts on this sentence should be ITALIC. You do it by simply inserting an em tag. The em tag stand for emphasis.

The texts on this sentence should be BOLD. In this one, you do it by using the strong tag.

You may also use both em and strong tags together to make bold italic texts. To do that, just put both tags inside your paragraph.
